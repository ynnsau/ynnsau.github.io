About

My name is Yan Sun, and I am a PhD student in ECE@UIUC, advised by Prof. Nam Sung Kim.

My research focuses on hardware-software co-design with the latest Compute eXpress Link (CXL) technology.

Before that, I received a BS degree in Computer Engieering with a minor in Statistic from UIUC in 2021.
